About Nisargam

From Nature, For You


Nisargam (निसर्गम) means coming from nature. We started this journey in Rashin with a simple mission: to provide chemical-free, skin-friendly soaps.

In a world full of synthetic detergents, our soaps are handmade in small batches using the traditional cold-process method. We use pure oils like Coconut, Olive, and Almond, infused with natural herbs.

Our promise is purity. No parabens, no sulfates, just pure love for your skin.
